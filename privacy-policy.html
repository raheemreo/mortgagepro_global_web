Welcome to Mortgage Pro Global ("Mortgage Pro Global", "we", "our", or "us").

This Privacy Policy explains how Mortgage Pro Global collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use:

  • the Mortgage Pro Global mobile application ("App"), and
  • the Mortgage Pro Global website ("Website").

By using our app or website, you agree to this privacy policy.

1. About Mortgage Pro Global

Mortgage Pro Global provides mortgage calculators, home loan tools, EMI calculators, refinancing calculators, affordability calculators, mortgage-related educational resources, lender information, and housing market reference information for multiple countries.

Mortgage Pro Global is an informational tool only.

We are not a bank, lender, mortgage broker, financial advisor, legal advisor, tax advisor, or investment advisor.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Our app does not require you to create an account to use core features.

We do not require you to provide:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Government identification
  • Bank information
  • Credit card information

unless you voluntarily contact us.

Calculator Inputs

Mortgage calculations are performed on your device.

Calculator inputs such as:

  • Loan amount
  • Interest rate
  • Property value
  • Down payment
  • Income
  • Mortgage term

are used only to generate calculation results.

These values are not transmitted to our servers.

Saved Favorites

If you choose to save calculators or tools, this information is stored locally on your device.

Analytics Information

With your consent where required by law, we use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used.

Analytics may include:

  • App version
  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • Screen views
  • App interactions
  • Crash diagnostics
  • Performance information
  • General geographic region (country or region)

We do not use Firebase Analytics to collect personally identifiable financial information.

Analytics collection remains disabled until consent is obtained where required.

You may withdraw consent at any time through the app's privacy settings.

Remote Configuration

We use Firebase Remote Config to improve app functionality, configure features, and deliver updates without requiring an application update.

Remote Config does not collect personal information.
