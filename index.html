About MortgagePro Global

MortgagePro Global is a free financial tool platform built by REO Technologies, a developer of trusted mortgage and loan calculator apps with over 50,000 downloads on Google Play. Our tools are built for accuracy, transparency, and accessibility — we use the standard compound interest formula (EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ - 1)) and source all rates from official central banks.

Our calculators cover the full range of home buying costs specific to each country: CMHC insurance premiums in Canada, Stamp Duty Land Tax in the UK, Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI) in Australia, PMI thresholds in the USA, and tax deduction benefits under India's Income Tax Act.

All calculations are estimates. Rates change daily — always verify with your lender or mortgage broker before making financial decisions. Read our full disclaimer.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • A mortgage calculator is a tool that estimates your monthly home loan payment based on the loan amount, interest rate, and repayment term. Professional calculators like those on MortgagePro Global also factor in country-specific costs such as taxes, insurance, and government fees (CMHC, Stamp Duty, LMI, PMI) to give a complete picture of your monthly and total home ownership cost.

  • MortgagePro Global calculators use the standard compound interest formula (EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ - 1)) and official central bank rates updated monthly. Results are estimates — actual payments may vary based on your lender's rate, fees, and specific loan product. Always confirm figures with a licensed mortgage broker or financial advisor before making decisions.

  • Use the calculator for the country where you are purchasing property, not where you live. Each country's calculator applies the correct local mortgage rules — for example, Canada uses semi-annual compounding (not monthly) and requires a stress test at 2% above your rate, while India applies EMI-based repayment with RBI repo rate-linked pricing. Choosing the wrong country will give you inaccurate results.
