Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: July 2026. Review how we manage data and how to request deletion.

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Mortgage Pro Global. We respect your privacy. We do not require users to create an account for core features and minimize personal data collection.

1. Data Processed

We process the following types of information when you interact with our services (with consent where required):

  • Analytics data (via Google Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Device identifiers used by third-party services
  • Advertising consent preferences
  • Locally stored app preferences

2. Data Not Stored

To ensure maximum privacy, Mortgage Pro Global does not collect or store sensitive personal or financial information, including:

  • Mortgage applications
  • Bank details or payment card information
  • Government-issued IDs
  • Mortgage balances
  • User accounts (since accounts are not required)

3. Local Device Data

Any settings or data saved directly inside our app or website is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Saved calculators and favorites
  • App settings and display themes
  • Country selections
  • Cached application content

You can easily remove this data by clearing the app storage (via device settings) or by uninstalling the application.

4. Data Deletion Requests

If you wish to submit a data deletion request for any information that may have been gathered (such as advertising identifiers or analytics logs), please contact us with the following details:

  • Email: privacy@mortgageproglobal.com
  • Subject Line: Data Deletion Request
  • Details to Include: Please specify your country of residence and a description of your request.

Important: Do not send sensitive financial information or government identification numbers in your email request.

5. Response Time

We aim to process and respond to data deletion requests within 30 days of receipt, subject to applicable local laws.

6. Retention

Certain information may be retained where required by law, to prevent fraud, resolve security issues, or enforce legal rights.

7. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services that may process certain data under their own privacy policies. These services include:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or need further clarification regarding this Data Deletion Policy, feel free to reach out to us:

  • Website: https://mortgageproglobal.com
  • Privacy: privacy@mortgageproglobal.com
  • Support: support@mortgageproglobal.com
  • Legal: legal@mortgageproglobal.com
